About Me

My name is Peter Filicetti. I graduated high school in June of 2018, and I have been working with HTML5, CSS3, and rudimentary JavaScript since the summer of 2017. My passion for computer and other various technologies comes primarily from my enjoyment of video games; however, after a thorough realization that I am terrible at just about every game out there, I figured there had to be some other way to get me in front of a computer screen without making a darn fool of myself.



In my free time, I like to go to the gym (regardless of how much that shows), watch various television shows on Netflix and Hulu, and bake some fantastic desserts for myself and my family. I also work part-time for my father, primarily in the way of leftover work that he does not have the time to work on, which is where I got my start in HTML and CSS.



My most noteworthy achievement (that is relevant to a professional portfolio) is a Microsoft certification from Exam 70-480 in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. I am working toward getting more certifications to register as a true Microsoft Certified Professional.
